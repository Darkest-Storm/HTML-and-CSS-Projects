Steps to make 2 farmers breakfast wraps

  1. Grab 2 frozen hashbrown patties and 2 frozen sausage patties
  2. Put the hashbrowns on top rack of air fryer and sausage on bottom
  3. Set the air fryer to 400 degrees for 15min and flip both the hashbrowns and sausages half way through cooking
  4. Scramble 3 eggs
  5. Grab 2 wraps and warm them in microsave for 30 seconds
  6. Put some chipolte sauce on the warm wraps
  7. Put half the scrambled eggs on each wrap
  8. Wait for air fryer to be done and place 1 hashbrown and 1 sausage patty on each wrap
  9. Fold the wraps and enjoy
